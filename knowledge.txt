Lionel Messi was born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina.
He joined FC Barcelona's youth academy La Masia at the age of 13.
Messi has won the Ballon d'Or award a record 8 times.
He won the FIFA World Cup with Argentina in 2022 in Qatar.
Messi joined Inter Miami in MLS in 2023.
He is considered by many as the greatest footballer of all time.
Messi has scored over 800 career goals across club and international football.
He won 10 La Liga titles with Barcelona.
Messi's shirt number at Inter Miami is 10.
Argentina won the Copa America in 2021 and 2024 with Messi as captain.
